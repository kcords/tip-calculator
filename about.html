About

This application was designed to provide a simple solution to the question of "How much is appropriate to tip?"

I have extracted recommended tipping values from reputable sources and consolidated them into pre-assigned tip percentages based on the quality of the service received.

You also have the ability to round the total amount to either the nearest dollar or quarter for cleaner record keeping.

Whether you're dining out or ordering takeout, this application will help you determine an appropriate tip amount with ease.

Thank you for using the application and feel free to let me know if you have any ideas or suggestions for new features.
